What we do

You sign. We speak.

Open the app and sign into the camera. Our AI watches your hands, recognizes Indian Sign Language gestures, turns them into natural sentences, and speaks them aloud — so the person in front of you hears you clearly. Real time. No delay.

How it works

Three steps to be heard

✋ 01

Sign into the camera

Use the mobile app and sign in Indian Sign Language

👁 02

AI recognizes gestures

Roboflow vision model detects ISL signs in real time

🔊 03

Instant speech

Gemini builds sentences, ElevenLabs speaks them aloud

For everyone

Built for real conversations

ISL Voice helps deaf and mute users communicate naturally — at home, at work, at the doctor’s office. No interpreter needed. Just sign, and the person in front of you hears your words.
